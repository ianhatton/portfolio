CSS

The art of presenting beautiful content for the web is something which still excites me to this day.

CSS is the area of my job which always makes the biggest initial impact and it's for this reason that I take great care in making sure that my responsive builds look fantastic and feel intuitive—no matter what platform they're being viewed on.

In my opinion, what's going on "under the hood" is just as important as what's presented in the browser, which is why I'm quite meticulous when it comes to organising my work and being as modular as possible. Spaghetti is good, but CSS spaghetti tastes bad.

I have made great relationships with the designers I have worked with in the past and I'm always happy to provide guidance and constructive criticism where it's due in order to deliver a stunning* final product.

*exact level of stunningness dependent on design.
