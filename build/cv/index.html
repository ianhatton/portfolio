Ian Hatton

Front-end Web Developer

A creative, forward-thinking Front-end Web Developer with over 5 years' agency experience. My strengths range from a thorough understanding of CSS and HTML, a proficiency in JavaScript and a firm grasp of web standards, typography and graphic design principles.

I am in my element whether I am hand-coding beautiful, bespoke designs, tailoring and using content management systems or working closely with clients to ensure that their ideas are realised to their full potential.

Objective

To put my skills into practice for a driven company and to add value across a range of projects. I seek to work in an environment which will inspire and encourage me to grow as a developer, whilst sharing and learning ideas alongside my peers.
